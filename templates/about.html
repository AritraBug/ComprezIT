About ComprezIT

ComprezIT is a powerful web-based tool designed to help you reduce the file size of your images while maintaining visual quality.

Fast & Efficient

Our compression algorithm optimizes your images quickly while preserving quality.

Secure

All images are processed locally and automatically removed after 24 hours.

Open Source

This tool is built with open source technologies and is free to use.

Key Features

  • Compress individual images with customizable quality
  • Batch processing for multiple files
  • Multiple output formats (JPG, PNG, WEBP)
  • Resize images by percentage or dimensions
  • Apply filters (blur, sharpen, grayscale)
  • Adjust brightness, contrast, and saturation
  • Option to remove EXIF data for privacy
  • View and download detailed compression results

How It Works

Image Compressor uses the powerful Python Imaging Library (PIL/Pillow) to optimize your images. The process works by:

  1. Analyzing your image's structure and metadata
  2. Applying intelligent compression algorithms based on your settings
  3. Optionally resizing and applying filters as requested
  4. Generating a new optimized file while preserving important image qualities
80% average reduction
Our users typically achieve 60-90% file size reduction while maintaining acceptable image quality.

Privacy Policy

We take your privacy seriously:

  • All uploaded images are processed locally on our servers
  • Images are automatically deleted after 24 hours
  • We do not share or use your images for any other purpose
  • No user accounts or personal information is collected
For sensitive images, consider using the "Remove EXIF Data" option to strip out potentially private metadata.
